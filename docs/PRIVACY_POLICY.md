# Pancake Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 27, 2026

Pancake is a running companion app that helps guide workouts and select music that fits the runner's current effort.

## What Pancake Uses

Pancake can request access to:

- Health data, such as workouts and heart rate, to guide runs and save completed workouts.
- Location, to measure outdoor run distance and pace.
- Apple Music library data, to learn the runner's music taste.
- Apple Music playback access, to play generated song suggestions from the Apple Music catalog.
- Notifications, to alert users about friend run activity and comments if social features are enabled.
- Sign in with Apple, to identify the user for optional friend activity features.

## How Data Is Handled

- Pancake stores run history, profile information, and music preferences on the device.
- Pancake stores social settings and preview friend activity on the device in the current build.
- Pancake does not include third-party advertising SDKs.
- Pancake does not use third-party analytics SDKs.
- Pancake does not sell personal information.
- Pancake does not track users across apps or websites.

## Apple Services

When Pancake searches the Apple Music catalog or requests playback through Apple frameworks, those requests are handled by Apple services under the user's Apple account and Apple Music subscription.

When Pancake reads or writes workout information through HealthKit, that data is managed through Apple's Health framework and the permissions granted by the user.

When Pancake uses Sign in with Apple, authentication is handled by Apple. The current build stores the resulting user identifier on device.

## User Controls

Users can:

- revoke Health access in the Health app or iPhone Settings
- revoke Location access in iPhone Settings
- revoke Apple Music library access in iPhone Settings
- revoke Apple Music playback access in iPhone Settings
- revoke notification access in iPhone Settings
- sign out of Pancake from the app when account controls are available
- clear stored profile and run-history data by deleting the app
